Welcome to the Stream

July 1, 2026 · meta

Most personal sites make you pick a lane: a blog, or a portfolio. I never worked that way — a weekend project usually turns into a blog post, and a half-formed idea from a blog post usually turns into a project. So instead of two separate sections pretending not to know about each other, everything lives in one chronological stream.

You’ll see a small mark next to each entry: a ✎ for something written, a ◐ for something built. That’s it — no categories to maintain, no decision paralysis about where a piece “belongs.”
